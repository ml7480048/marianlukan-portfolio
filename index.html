03 — About

Marian Lukan

Producer with extensive experience across commercial, television and film production in Austria and Ukraine. Based in Vienna since 2022, running full-cycle commercial video production — 70+ productions for brands and agencies, in close collaboration with Vienna's leading advertising agencies.

Before Vienna, built a 15-year career in Kyiv: leading production at GVARDIYA Production House, running post-production for INTER's flagship social TV show, executive-producing feature films, and — for six years — producing entertainment and news formats across 1+1, ICTV, INTER, Pilot Studio and MTV Ukraine, including shows such as "I Am President," "National Star" and "Podrobnosti nedeli." Also served as Head of Department at the Ukrainian State Film Agency, overseeing national film funding and Ukraine's EURIMAGES membership application.

Recently focused on integrating AI tools into production workflows and building vertical-format content for digital platforms.
